Canada Federal Bot

Description:

This bot via the OpenParliment API provides Federal political information for Canada. There are currently five main supported topics:

  • Bills: Statuses of bills within Canadian history including both government and private memebr bills
  • Votes: Votes of bills within Canadian history with details such as number of 'YEA' vs 'NAY' and if the bill was passed or defeated
  • Member of Parliment (MP): List of current Canadian MPs along with the riding and party they represent
  • Debates: Debates held within the House of Commons and their topics
  • Committees: List of committees and in which sessions were they active

How to Use:

Start with the START button below! Using one of the five mentioned topics above, you may enter filter criterias for particular bills, vote counts, member of parliments, debates or committees

Examples:

  • Look up bill C-11: An act to amend the Canadian Broadcasting Act
  • Find the status of bill C-294 within the 44-1 session
  • Find the riding represented by the The Right Honourable Chrystia Freeland
  • Look at the recent debate on December 12, 2022
  • See when the Ethics committee last met
